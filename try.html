IEEE-754 Binary-32 Floating Point Converter

This page allows you to convert binary numbers to the IEEE 754 32-bit base-2 floating-point format and also show the Hexadecimal representation

Usage: Enter the significand and exponent of the binary number to the corresponding input box

You can enter special keywords such as "NaN", "0", "-0", "Inf", "Infinity", "-Inf", "-Infinity" in the Significand input box

Only binary inputs and special case words are allowed
